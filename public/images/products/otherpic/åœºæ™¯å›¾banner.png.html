How to Cooperate

Project Evaluation

The location of the project, the condition of the land, the investment amount, whether it is for tourism or resort,or Airbnb, whether the project is for personal investment or institutional investment, and the calculation of investment return rate

Project Planning

Cultural and tourism real estate development requires a clear project positioning, product planning,design, and Feasibility report for housing selection, project risk report, environmental assessment report, and investment return ratebased on the positioning

Product and project licenses
Provide various customizable products based on local licenses and certifications，Depending on the complexity of the project, the production process takes 30-50 days. Each client can visit the production process of the house and see that we strictly adhere to standards and ensure the quality of our work.
Foundation Preparation

While your house is being manufactured, you need to prepare the foundation for it at the same time. We will provide you with detailed instructions and the necessary drawings. At the same time, plan water, electricity and other systems in advance.


Delivery and Installation
The house can be delivery in the whole house or parts. You can arrange for a freight forwarder to deliver it. If deliveryed in the whole house, the installation is very simple and can be done in a few hours. If deliveryed in house parts, you can assemble it with detailed drawings and video instructions or use our partners to assemble it.
